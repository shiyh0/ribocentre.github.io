## Description
***

<font size=4>The Vg1 ribozyme is the smallest ribozyme to be identified which is a manganese-dependent ribozyme. The Vg1 ribozyme catalyzes a first-order reaction where its mechanism of cleavage is similar to the manganese ribozyme present in Tetrahymena group I introns.</font><br>
